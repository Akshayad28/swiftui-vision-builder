---

### **Issue Type:** Bug

### **Summary:**

Incorrect handling of sub-security categories KD and KC in Derivatives (Long/Short) for Forms 8.3 & 8.5

---

### **Description:**

As Forms 8.3 and 8.5 are not getting generated via the RNS UI, we used backend SQL queries (`form_8.3_section_3b` and `form_8.5_section_3b`) to validate the sub-security category scenarios.

Using these queries, we tested all four scenarios related to addition and removal of sub-security categories.

Out of the four scenarios:

* **SA, PB, FA included in Long – Options** → ✅ Passed
* **SA, PB, FA included in Short – Options** → ✅ Passed

However, issues were observed in the below two scenarios:

---

### **Scenario 1:**

**KA, KB, KD excluded from Long – Derivatives**

#### **Expected Result:**

* KA, KB, KD should be removed from **Long – Derivatives**
* KD should still be present in **Short – Derivatives**

#### **Actual Result:**

* KA and KB are correctly removed from both Forms 8.3 and 8.5
* However, **KD trades are completely removed from both Long and Short Derivatives**
* KD is **not present in Short Derivatives**, which is incorrect

---

### **Scenario 2:**

**KA, KB, KC excluded from Short – Derivatives**

#### **Expected Result:**

* KA, KB, KC should be removed from **Short – Derivatives**
* KC should still be present in **Long – Derivatives**

#### **Actual Result:**

* KA and KB are correctly removed from both Forms 8.3 and 8.5
* However, **KC trades are present in both Long and Short Derivatives**
* KC is **not removed from Short Derivatives**, which is incorrect

---

### **Environment:**

* Region: UK
* Module: UK Takeover Panel – RNS Reporting
* Validation Method:

  * `form_8.3_section_3b`
  * `form_8.5_section_3b`

---

### **Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Insert test data for sub-security categories (KA, KB, KC, KD, SA, PB, FA)
2. Update reporting flags (`RPRT_83_FLAG`, `RPRT_85_FLAG`)
3. Execute queries:

   * `form_8.3_section_3b`
   * `form_8.5_section_3b`
4. Validate Long and Short Derivatives data for each sub-security category

---

### **Expected Result (Summary):**

* KD should appear in **Short – Derivatives only**
* KC should appear in **Long – Derivatives only**

---

### **Actual Result (Summary):**

* KD is removed from both Long and Short Derivatives
* KC is present in both Long and Short Derivatives

---

### **Impact:**

* Incorrect implementation of sub-security category filtering logic
* Partial failure of regulatory reporting scenarios
* Risk of incorrect data in Takeover Panel reports
